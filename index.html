VISION

Saint Anthony Academy of Quezon City shall continually transform as a propellant of development and a venue for leaders of learning who will involve complex interaction in enhancing the growth of individuals who are capable of logical thinking and moral judgement. The school shall provide an atmospgere that will induce scholarly pursuits so that our graduates can live a satisfying life, provide leadership as required by our society, and achieve his final destiny and ultimate end which is glorifying God, his Creator.

MISSION

Saint Anthony Academy of Quezon City shall provide for the essential educational foundation that will develop their maximum potential to be morally upright, productive, versatile, and responsive to the changing needs and condition of the nation. It aims to furnish holistic formation of well-equipped individuals that will provide leadership in the advancement of knowledge for improving the quality of human life.
